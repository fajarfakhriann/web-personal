Frequently Asked Questions

Let’s clear up a few things you might be wondering about.

How much does a website cost?

The cost depends on the complexity and features you need. A basic website (such as a landing page or portfolio) starts from IDR 5.500.000. For personal web or custom systems, I’ll provide a quote after discussing the requirements.

How long does it take to build a website?

It usually takes around 1 to 4 weeks, depending on the features and number of revisions. A detailed timeline will be given after I receive the complete brief.

Can I request a custom design?

Absolutely! You can share your own design ideas or references. I can also create a custom UI/UX design for you using tools like Figma or Canva.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, every website I build is fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

Can I request revisions after the website is done?

Yes, I offer 2 free revisions after project completion. Additional revisions can be done at a low extra cost.

Is there any warranty or after-launch support?

Yes. I provide a 1-month warranty for bug fixes. For long-term support or website maintenance, we can arrange an ongoing service plan.

Do you help with domain and hosting?

Yes, I can help you register a domain, choose the right hosting, and handle all setup so your website goes live smoothly.

How do we start the project?

You can contact me via the contact form or email on this website. We’ll begin with a discussion to understand your needs, I’ll send a quote, and once approved, we’ll kick off the project right away.
